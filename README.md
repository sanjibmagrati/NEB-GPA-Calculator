Hello! This website tool is made to help all Nepali students easily calculate their final NEB Class 12 results.

The National Examination Board (NEB) uses a specific grading system (like the one used from 2025/2026). Doing the calculation can be confusing and slow. This simple online tool removes all the guesswork! Just put in your marks or grades, and it instantly gives you your accurate Grade Point Average (GPA).

It is designed to be your quick, reliable helper for checking results and preparing reports.

âœ¨ Why You Need This Tool (Key Benefits)
This calculator is more than just a simple math tool. It is built to make your academic life easier:
 1. Fast & Accurate GPA: You get your final GPA right away. The calculation follows the official NEB Grade Point rules perfectly, so you can trust the result.
 2. Total Subject Control: Don't have the standard subjects? No problem! You can easily add, remove, or change any subject on the list. This makes sure the calculation is 100% specific to your own results sheet.
 3. Flexible Input: You choose the easiest way to enter your scores:
 4. Enter Marks: Type in your numerical marks (e.g., 78/100). The tool converts them to the right Grade Point.
 5. Select Grades: Simply select the grade you got (A+, B+, C).
 6. Create a Report (PDF Ready): When the GPA is ready, one click creates a clean, professional report. You can easily print this report or save it as a PDF file to keep for your records, share with parents, or send to colleges.

ðŸ’» How to Use It (Step-by-Step Guide)
 - Using the calculator is very easy and takes just a few clicks:
 - Start the Tool: Open the website (or just double-click the index.html file if you are running it from your computer).
 - Select Your Stream: First, select your study stream (like Science, Management, Humanities, etc.). This loads your common subjects.
 - Choose Input Style: Decide if you want to enter Marks or Grades for all your subjects.
 - Manage Subjects: If you have any extra optional subjects, use the "Add Subject" button to include them in the calculation.
 - Enter Your Results: Type or select your score for every subject displayed on the screen.
 - Get Your GPA: Click the big "Calculate GPA" button. Your final GPA, analysis, and overall performance feedback will show up below.
 - Save/Print: Click the "Save Results" button. A new window will appear with the report. Use your browser's print option and choose "Save as PDF" to keep a digital copy.
